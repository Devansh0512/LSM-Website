ARC Studios

We aim to start a game development studio in which we will be developing and publishing some arcade games. We will also be developing a website that will consist of all our developed games and the games will be monitored and updated regularly according to the customer needs and the technological growth. Currently our gaming studio will work on a small scale,but in the future, seeing the trends and revenue generation for our studio we will develop more games. As and when our enterprise grows and becomes more recognized, we aim to spread our reach all over the world. We have created the first few games for kick starting our gaming studio. The games are designed for Windows and android operating systems. With games that could be freely downloaded through PCs on the web, just like any other piece of software, the free-to-play model has become widely popular among digital games.
